Study Visa<\/h2>\n

\n A Study Visa belongs to the non-immigrant visa category and is made available for those students who are willing and eligible to study abroad for their higher studies. This visa is called F1 Visa in the USA and Study Permit in Canada. Intakes or semesters for different universities and countries may vary from each other. Once a student successfully obtains this visa, he\/she can study in his\/her favorite educational institution in the desired country.\n <\/p>\n<\/p><\/div>\n

What do we mean by Intakes?<\/h2>\n

\n Intakes are nothing but semesters. Every country offers different intakes to students. Each intake has specific durations in which the students study in the relevant program in the country. It’s up to the student’s choice of the intake, the availability of the selected program, the academic scores of the student, the English proficiency scores of the student, etc. on the basis of which the student can select his\/her program semester or intake.\n <\/p>\n<\/p><\/div>\n
