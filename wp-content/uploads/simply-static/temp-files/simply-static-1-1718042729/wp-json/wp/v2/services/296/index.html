Pearson Test of English Academic (PTE)<\/h2>\n

\n PTE, also known as the Pearson Test of English Academic, is one of the most trusted English Aptitude Tests all over the world that Pearson Inc. conducts for work, study, and visa purposes, undertaken by students and aspirants who desire to go abroad to study or immigrate to a major English-speaking country. Unlike IELTS, this test consists of three sections: Speaking and Writing (Combined), Reading, and Listening. Another difference is that this exam is computer-based whereas IELTS is both computer- and paper-based. <\/p>\n
