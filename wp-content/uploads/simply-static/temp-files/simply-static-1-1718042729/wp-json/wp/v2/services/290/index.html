Canada PR<\/h2>\n

\n A PR visa, or Permanent Resident Visa, allows you to travel to a nation, stay for a period of time, and then seek citizenship. In some countries, obtaining a PR visa eventually leads to citizenship\n <\/p>\n

\n When someone obtains a Permanent Resident Visa, they get the right to live in the country for a specific period of time (5 years in case of Canada). They are still citizens of the other country; but now they are called permanent residents of the country they immigrated to. What’s important to note here is that a person with a study visa or work permit can also stay in the country for a specific time period but are not called Permanent Residents unless they acquire that visa after being eligible for the same.\n <\/p>\n<\/p><\/div>\n
