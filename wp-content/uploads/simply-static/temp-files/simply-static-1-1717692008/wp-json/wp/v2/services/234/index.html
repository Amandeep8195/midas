Pearson Test of English Academic (PTE)<\/h2>\n

\n PTE, also known as the Pearson Test of English Academic, is one of the most trusted English Aptitude Tests all over the world that Pearson Inc. conducts for work, study, and visa purposes. Unlike IELTS, this test consists of three sections: Speaking and Writing (Combined), Reading, and Listening. Another difference is that this exam is computer-based whereas IELTS is both computer- and paper-based.\n <\/p>\n

\n Midas Touch aims at preparing students to take all test sections of PTE confidently by honing their 360-degree skills in the English Language. Our trainers are well-versed with the fact that your knowledge of the language might be basic but it has considerable scope to expand itself to the highest realms.\n <\/p>\n<\/p><\/div>\n
