Spouse Visa\/Dependent Visa<\/h2>\n

\n To live in your desired country with your spouse, parents, and kids, you need to get a dependent visa for them. Whether you’re a student, a working professional, or a permanent resident in that country, you can apply for a dependent visa for the person you want to take there along with you.\n <\/p>\n

\n Depending on the country, getting a Dependent or Spouse Visa can be challenging as it requires a lot of documentation of the person in question. You also need a Dependent Certificate to prove that you are eligible to be granted a Dependent\/ Spouse Visa. This certificate mainly applies to people who are working but still need to stay with the other person for basic needs such as food and shelter.\n <\/p>\n<\/p><\/div>\n
